About Me

My name is Theo Halpern and I currently go to West Seattle High school. I went to middle school at Seattle Country Day School and I’m doing this reading blog for My Intro to Literary Studies class with Mr. Wiener (I swear, that's his real name). I like reading, but I have always found it hard thanks to my dyslexia. In elementary school I would listen to many audio books, I have 1.6 months on audible and I only started that in middle school. Before then I would go to the libear about once a week and take home two to three audio books and listen to them on my CD player. This is when I gained a real love for storytelling. Some of my favorite books though are a little more factual, one being The Infographic History of the World. When I can’t find anything else to read I can always learn something new about the universe.
